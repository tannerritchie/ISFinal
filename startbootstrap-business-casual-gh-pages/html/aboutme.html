My Mission

From the summer of 2019 to the summer of 2021 I was a full-time volunteer missionary for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. The first half of that two years was spent in Indonesia, and the second half in Upstate New York.

As a missionary, my purpose was to "invite others to come unto Christ by helping them receive the restored gospel through faith in Jesus Christ and His Atonement, repentance, baptism, receiving the gift of the Holy Ghost, and enduring to the end." Basically, I tried to help as many people as I could come closer to Jesus Christ.


I am so grateful for the chance that I had to serve a mission. My mission changed my life and I wouldn't trade those two years for the world.
